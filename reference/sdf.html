class NeuroMechFly.sdf.units.SimulationUnitScaling(meters=1, seconds=1, kilograms=1)¶

Bases: NeuroMechFly.utils.options.Options

Simulation scaling

1 [m] in reality = self.meterss [m] in simulation 1 [s] in reality = self.seconds [s] in simulation 1 [kg] in reality = self.kilograms [kg] in simulation

acceleration¶

Acceleration

Scaled as self.gravity = self.meters/self.seconds**2

density¶

Density

Scaled as self.density = self.kilograms/self.meters**3

gravity¶

Gravity

Scaled as self.gravity = self.meters/self.seconds**2

hertz¶

Hertz (frequency)

Scaled as self.hertz = 1/self.seconds

newtons¶

Newtons

Scaled as self.newtons = self.kilograms*self.meters/self.time**2

torques¶

Torques

Scaled as self.torques = self.kilograms*self.meters**2/self.time**2

velocity¶

Velocity

Scaled as self.velocities = self.meters/self.seconds

volume¶

Volume

Scaled as self.volume = self.meters**3
